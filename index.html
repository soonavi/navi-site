The leak

Most businesses lose money before lunch.

It is rarely a marketing problem. It is a follow-through problem. The call comes in and nobody picks up. The lead fills out a form and waits two days for a reply. By then they have already hired someone else.

!

The missed call. 1 in 4 calls to small businesses go unanswered, and most callers never try twice.

!

The slow reply. A lead is far more likely to book the business that answers first, not the best one.

!

The forgotten quote. Quotes sit in an inbox with no follow-up, and the job quietly goes cold.
